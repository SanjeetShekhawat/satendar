INFRASTRUCTURE

Our Factory Premises

Our industry leading technologies and machinery span multiple manufacturing units across West Bengal, totalling 115000 square feet of manufacturing space. Our plants specialize in various metal manufacturing techniques such as Laser Cutting, Pressing, Forming, Welding and Surface Treatment. We are willing to enter into collaborations for producing world class products at much economical costs.

Our offering of subcontract sheet metal fabrication services along with our team of highly experienced engineers, ensure that we do full justice to our decades old brand value.
