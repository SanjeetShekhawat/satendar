We at Seizo Technology have 2 Fiber Laser machines that offer high versatility and run lights out, 24-hours-per-day, seven-days-a-week to deliver an array of sheet metal components and assemblies for various industries.

Our Bystronic Fiber Laser machines cut materials with a repetitive accuracy of 0.03 mm, including Mild Steel, Stainless Steel, Aluminium Alloys, Galvanized Steel, Titanium, Brass and Copper in sheet sizes up to 2050 mm X 4100 mm.

Laser Cutting operations on our 6000 Watt machine ensures highly accurate cut parts with no heat damage and cut quality so good that it can be used for cutting very intricate shapes, elaborate patterns and small holes.

Our Machine Capacity is as follows:

  • 1. Maximum Bed Size: 2050 mm X 4100 mm
  • 2. Stainless Steel Cutting upto 30 mm Thick
  • 3. Aluminium cutting upto 30 mm Thick
  • 4. Mild Steel cutting upto 25 mm Thick
  • 5. Brass cutting upto 15 mm Thick
  • 6. Copper, Galvanized Sheets and Titanium cutting upto 12 mm Thick

We have Two CNC Press Brakes and specialize in Metal Folding and Fabrication services. We deliver products which are reliable, high on productivity and predictable lead times with the use of robust software on Delem Controllers.

Laser Marking Services

At Seizo Technology, we specialize in laser marking, offering precise and permanent marking solutions for a wide range of materials. Our advanced laser systems ensure clear, high-quality markings on metals, plastics, ceramics, and more, providing accurate identification and branding for various industries.

 

Precision Laser Marking

Utilizing state-of-the-art laser technology, we can mark components with high precision and fine detail. Whether it’s logos, barcodes, serial numbers, or any other custom markings, our lasers provide durable, long-lasting results that meet the specific needs of our clients.

We also offer custom laser marking solutions, assisting clients from initial design through to the final product. Our expertise ensures that every mark is made with exceptional accuracy, creating clear and readable markings that last throughout the lifetime of the part.

 

At Seizo Technology, we offer Wood Engraving services, specializing in intricate designs and custom engravings for wood products. Using state-of-the-art laser engraving technology, we deliver precise, high-quality engravings on various types of wood, from hardwoods to softwoods. Our team ensures that every engraving meets the highest standards of detail and accuracy.

We engrave a range of products including Furniture, Signage, Decorative Panels, Awards, and Custom Designs. With years of experience, we are trusted by multiple industries to create unique and durable wood engravings that bring designs to life.

We are also capable of engraving large-scale projects, from intricate designs to corporate branding, and we offer additional services such as polishing, staining, and finishing to enhance the final product. Our attention to detail ensures every project is completed with the utmost care and precision.

 

Our metal welding facilities include highly skilled MIG and TIG Welding for an extensive range of ferrous and non ferrous materials. Our highly experienced Welding team is EN 15085 and IS 3834-2 certified and specialize in welding of low to medium gauge thick material, and are trained to a very high standard. We manufacture multiple types of products with vast ranging complexities and sizes. Our products are suitable for applications where quality is of prime importance and tolerance control is critical.

 

Industrial Fabrication

At Seizo Technology, we specialize in industrial fabrication, offering a wide range of services to manufacture large and small components. Our capabilities include precision metal fabrication, welding, cutting, and assembly, ensuring high-quality production for various industries.

 

Structural Fabrication

Our state-of-the-art fabrication equipment enables us to efficiently create complex metal structures with high precision. We fabricate components using materials such as Carbon Steel, Stainless Steel, Aluminum, and other metals to meet the exact specifications of our customers.

We also offer comprehensive solutions for custom fabrication projects, helping clients with everything from initial design to the final assembly of metal parts. Our expertise ensures that every product is manufactured to the highest standards of quality and performance.

 

Powder Coating and metal finishing facilities at Seizo Technology enable us to offer high-quality protective coatings for a wide range of components. We specialize in applying durable powder coatings that provide excellent resistance to wear, corrosion, and UV exposure, ensuring long-lasting finishes for metal parts.

We work with a network of ISO certified suppliers and associates to provide services such as Powder Coating, Finish Painting, Zinc Plating, Hot Dip Galvanizing, and more. All our powder coating processes are rigorously inspected to ensure the highest quality standards before parts are moved to further stages like Wood Engraving or dispatched to clients.
