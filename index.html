India's Leading Sheet Metal Fabricator

Seizo Technology is a leading provider of advanced engineering and fabrication solutions in India, specializing in precision sheet metal fabrication and Laser Markingservices for large Original Equipment Manufacturers (OEMs). With multiple manufacturing facilities across India, Seizo Technology serves a wide range of industries, including Railways, Defence, Switchgear, Kiosk & Vending, Automotive, Architecture, and Food Processing. This broad industry expertise enables Seizo Technology to effectively meet diverse subcontract engineering and metalworking demands.

Our comprehensive services include CNC Laser Cutting, CNC Machining, Metal Forming, Welding, Powder Coating, and specialized Stainless Steel Fabrication.
