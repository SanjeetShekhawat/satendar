ABOUT US

INTRODUCTION

Fabricators India, an ISO 9001:2015 certified engineering firm, was established in 1979, to provide complete fabrication solutions to the sheet metal industry for India and Globally. Fabricators India is one of the largest suppliers of Aluminium & Stainless Steel Water Tanks and Enclosures for the Indian Railways. Our other accreditations include ISO 3834-2 and EN 15085 certifications.

TEAM

We have a strong team of management graduates and engineering support to provide the requisite technical expertise in the field of Sheet Metal Fabrication and Mechanical Engineering, and Quality Control.

Spread over 2 units in Howrah, West Bengal in the eastern part of India, Fabricators India has all the CNC and NC machines for the precision needs and high productivity required.

We have Bystronic 6KW Fiber Laser Profile Cutting Machines, CNC Lathes, CNC Press Brakes, and TIG/MIG Welding in-house. We also have access to Surface Coating units and Painting facilities. We have access to Kolkata port which helps us ship products globally.
